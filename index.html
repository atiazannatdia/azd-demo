Risk & Mitigation

Offshore annotation has real risks. We make them explicit and mitigate them by design.

Risk: Data leakage Medical data requires strict operational controls.
Mitigation Restricted devices, access control, audit logs, and no uncontrolled transfer.
Risk: Annotation inconsistency Different annotators interpret labels differently.
Mitigation Clear definitions, calibration rounds, gold datasets, and dual-review QC.
Risk: Communication gaps Requirements get lost across time zones and language.
Mitigation Japan-time checkpoints, concise spec templates, escalation rules, weekly QA reports.
Risk: Infrastructure instability Power/internet issues can slow delivery.
Mitigation Redundancy planning (UPS/generator) and schedule-buffered operations.
